Who are we?

Vicious Cycles is a bike store located in the Raleigh, North Carolina. We offers products to those who are looking for a casual bike ride or need a bike that can transport items. The bikes we have are cruisers and cargo bikes. We sell parts and accessories for our bikes and offer repair services for our customers on both types of bikes.

Philosophy

Our philosophy is that when you choose your bike, you should choose it based on the folks who will not only build your bicycle, but also those who will help you get comfortable on the bike, make sure it suites your needs, as well as provide service down the road as you need it. The best things in life are pretty simple. Riding bikes helps us to disconnect from the hectic and noisy world and connect with friends, nature and ourselves. It might just be the best thing ever.
